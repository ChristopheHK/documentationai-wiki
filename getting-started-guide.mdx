## Understand Hub Médiation

Hub Médiation by Avosmarc lets you resolve disputes online with the help of certified mediators in France, without going to court.

Use the platform to:

- **File a mediation request** for a consumer or commercial dispute.
- **Share your situation and documents** securely with the mediator.
- **Follow the progress of the mediation** from your dashboard, alongside email notifications.

## What you need before you start

<Callout kind="info">

Have these elements ready before creating your account and filing a request:

- A valid email address you can access immediately.
- Basic information about the dispute: people or organizations involved, dates, amounts, and key events.
- Any useful documents: contracts, invoices, emails, letters, or previous attempts to resolve the issue.

</Callout>

Arriving with these details prepared makes the online form faster to complete and helps the mediator understand your case from the first review.

## Choose your path

Decide whether you are starting as a user who wants to file a mediation request or as a mediator who wants to join the network.

### Onboarding as a user: step-by-step

<Steps>

<Step title="Create your account" icon="rocket">

Go to the registration page at `https://avosmarc.fr/mediateur/inscription` in your browser.

- Enter your personal details and email address.
- Choose a secure password and accept the platform terms.

After you submit the form, you see a message indicating that a confirmation email has been sent.

</Step>

<Step title="Confirm your email and log in" icon="check-circle">

Open your inbox and look for the confirmation message from Avosmarc.

- Click the activation link in the email to confirm your account.
- After confirmation, go to the login page at `https://avosmarc.fr/login`.
- Enter your email and password to access your dashboard.

You know this step worked when your browser shows your Hub Médiation dashboard instead of the login screen.

</Step>

<Step title="Start a mediation request" icon="plus">

From your dashboard, use the button labeled `Déposer une demande de médiation` to open the request form.

- Confirm that you are starting a new mediation.
- Read any on-screen guidance about eligibility or required information.

The new request form opens, and you can move on to describing your dispute.

</Step>

<Step title="Describe your dispute" icon="edit">

Fill in the fields about your situation as precisely as possible.

- Identify the other party (person or organization).
- Indicate the nature of the dispute, relevant dates, and any amounts at stake.
- Summarize what happened and what you are seeking from the mediation.

Your progress bar or form indicators show when all mandatory fields are complete.

</Step>

<Step title="Upload supporting documents" icon="upload">

Attach the documents that help explain or prove your situation.

- Add contracts, invoices, quotations, written exchanges, or official notices.
- Check that each document is readable before confirming the upload.

Once uploaded, the list of documents appears in your request, and you can open each file to verify it.

</Step>

<Step title="Review and submit your request" icon="check-circle">

Read through your request one last time.

- Correct any missing or inaccurate information.
- Confirm that all key documents are attached.

Submit the form to send your request to Hub Médiation. You see an on-screen confirmation that your mediation request has been recorded.

</Step>

</Steps>

### What happens after you submit

After submission, Hub Médiation:

- Sends a **confirmation email** to the address linked to your account.
- Shares your request with a **certified mediator in France**, based on the type of dispute.
- Guides the next steps, which may include contacting the other party and proposing meeting formats (online or other).

You follow the status from your dashboard and through follow-up emails, without needing to contact the court directly.

</Tab>

### Onboarding as a mediator

If you are a professional mediator, use this path to join the Avosmarc network on Hub Médiation.

<Callout kind="info">

Before you register as a mediator, prepare:

- A valid **mediation certification** or equivalent proof of training.
- Your professional contact details and any registration numbers required in your activity area.
- A few lines describing your experience and areas of specialization.

</Callout>

<Steps>

<Step title="Check your prerequisites" icon="info">

Confirm that you hold a valid mediation certification and meet the practice requirements in your jurisdiction.

If you are unsure which documents are expected, review the guidance in the mediator documentation for more detail.

</Step>

<Step title="Register as a mediator" icon="plus">

Go to the mediator registration page at `https://avosmarc.fr/mediateur/inscription`.

- Enter your personal and professional information.
- Indicate your mediation certification details.
- Create login credentials if you do not already have an account.

Registration usually takes a few minutes when you have your information ready.

</Step>

<Step title="Validate your account and log in" icon="check-circle">

Check your inbox for the Avosmarc confirmation email.

- Click the activation link to validate your account.
- Log in at `https://avosmarc.fr/login` with your credentials.

After logging in, you access the mediator view of Hub Médiation, where you can see or receive assigned cases.

</Step>

</Steps>

For detailed information about expectations, profile configuration, and how case assignment works, see the [Mediator Guide](/mediator-guide).

</Tab>
