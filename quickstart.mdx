## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A valid email address for account creation
- Basic information about your dispute (parties involved, description)
- Access to a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari recommended)
</Callout>

<Callout kind="tip">
Hub Médiation resolves disputes quickly and affordably. Prepare your case details to streamline the process.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes to access the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to the [registration page](https://avosmarc.fr/mediateur/inscription) or the main login portal.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, full name, and create a strong password. Agree to the terms of service.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from Hub Médiation and click to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the [login page](https://avosmarc.fr/login) and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Submit Your First Mediation Request

Once logged in, file your request effortlessly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Start a New Request" icon="plus">
        Click "Déposer une demande de médiation" from the dashboard.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Fill the Form" icon="file-text">
        Enter dispute details: parties, description, and preferred mediator location.
      </Step>

      <Step title="Review and Submit" icon="check-circle">
        Preview your request and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the companion app and follow the same form-based process for on-the-go submissions.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

For advanced users integrating via API, here's a sample request:

<CodeGroup tabs="cURL,JavaScript">
````bash
curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/mediation-requests \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "dispute": {
      "parties": ["Party A", "Party B"],
      "description": "Contract dispute over payment terms",
      "location": "France"
    }
  }'
````

````javascript
const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/mediation-requests', {
  method: 'POST',
  headers: {
    'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
    'Content-Type': 'application/json'
  },
  body: JSON.stringify({
    dispute: {
      parties: ['Party A', 'Party B'],
      description: 'Contract dispute over payment terms',
      location: 'France'
    }
  })
});
````
</CodeGroup>

<ParamField path="dispute.parties" param-type="array" required="true">
Array of involved parties.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="dispute.description" param-type="string" required="true">
Detailed description of the dispute.
</ParamField>
