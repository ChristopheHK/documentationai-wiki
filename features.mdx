## Mediation Workflow

Follow this structured process to resolve disputes amicably.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Request" icon="file-plus">
    Fill out the online form with dispute details, parties involved, and supporting documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mediator Matching" icon="search">
    The platform matches your case with qualified mediators from the national directory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Virtual Sessions" icon="video">
    Schedule and conduct sessions via secure video calls or chat.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agreement Signing" icon="check-circle">
    Draft and e-sign binding agreements directly in the platform.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Track progress in your dashboard at all stages for full transparency.
</Callout>

## Pricing and Tariffs

Choose cost-effective options tailored to your needs.

| Plan       | Description                          | Fee Structure          |
|------------|--------------------------------------|------------------------|
| Basic     | Simple disputes `<5000€`             | Fixed `€99` per case  |
| Standard  | Medium complexity `5000-25000€`      | `2%` of claim value   |
| Premium   | Complex cases `>25000€`              | Custom quote          |

<Callout kind="info">
  All plans include unlimited sessions until resolution. No win, no fee for unsuccessful mediations.
</Callout>
