## Joining the Avosmarc Network

As a certified mediator, you can join the Avosmarc network to access client requests from across France. Registration connects you to the national directory and enables case management through the Hub Médiation platform.

<Callout kind="info">
  Ensure you hold a valid mediation certification before registering. The process takes about 5 minutes.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to the [mediator registration page](https://avosmarc.fr/mediateur/inscription).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Out Form" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your professional details, certification number, areas of expertise, and contact information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and Verify" icon="check-circle">
    Submit the form and check your email for verification. Activate your account to access the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in at [https://avosmarc.fr/login](https://avosmarc.fr/login), you land on the mediator dashboard. It provides a centralized view of your profile, incoming requests, active sessions, and analytics.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Profile Management" icon="user">
    Update your bio, availability, hourly rates, and expertise tags. Add documents like certifications for client trust.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Request Inbox" icon="inbox">
    View new mediation requests matching your profile. Filter by dispute type, location, or urgency.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sessions" icon="calendar">
    Schedule and track mediation sessions. Log outcomes, generate reports, and invoice clients directly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Client Requests and Sessions

Handle requests efficiently to resolve disputes amicably. Respond promptly to build your reputation on the platform.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Review Requests" icon="search" href="#">
    Assess request details including parties involved, dispute summary, and proposed timelines.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accept or Decline" icon="check-circle" href="#">
    Accept suitable cases or politely decline others. Provide reasons for transparency.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Schedule Sessions" icon="calendar" href="#">
    Propose dates via the built-in scheduler. Coordinate with all parties.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conduct Mediation" icon="video" href="#">
    Use secure video calls or in-person notes. Document agreements in real-time.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Respond to Request" icon="reply">
    Reply within 24 hours. Outline your approach and fees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prepare Session" icon="file-text">
    Review case files and prepare neutral questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finalize Outcome" icon="award">
    Draft the mediation agreement. Upload signed documents to close the case.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices for Successful Mediations

Follow these guidelines to maximize resolution rates and client satisfaction.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="Communication Tips" default-open="true">
    Listen actively without interrupting. Use open-ended questions to uncover underlying interests. Maintain neutrality at all times.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Documentation">
    Record key agreements verbatim. Use platform templates for consistency and legal compliance.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Follow-Up">
    Check in post-session to ensure implementation. Offer additional sessions if needed.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Track your success metrics in the dashboard to refine your approach. High-resolution rates improve your visibility in the directory.
</Callout>
